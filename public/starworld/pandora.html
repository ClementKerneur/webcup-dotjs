ANDROID LAND

The planet of Pandora in a far distant system have Navi's as care takers. The universe is based on the movie of James Cameroon called "Avatar". The landscape, animals, plants and navi's have incredible abilities and a unique appearance. If you love mother nature then Pandora will be the paradise you have always dreamed of. The Android guides is composed of a huntress Navi, Jake Sully one of the lead actor in the movie and Neytiri the soulmate of Jake Sully.
